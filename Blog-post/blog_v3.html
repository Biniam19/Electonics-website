How to Fix a Broken Back Glass on Your Phone

Cracked back glass? Learn why professional replacement is recommended and how Efix restores your phone’s look and function safely.

Step 1 — Assess the damage

Small hairline cracks are often cosmetic. Deeper fractures risk letting moisture or debris reach wireless charging coils or camera modules.

Step 2 — Don’t attempt risky DIY

Modern phones use strong adhesives and fragile internal parts. DIY removal without proper heat tools can damage cameras, antennas, or the battery.

Step 3 — Professional heat-and-replace

Efix uses calibrated heat and precision tools to separate glued panels, replace with OEM-quality glass, and reseal correctly so water resistance is preserved where possible.

Step 4 — Protect it going forward

Use a case with raised edges or a clear shock-absorbent back cover to maintain the phone’s aesthetic while protecting it from new drops.
