How to Fix a Broken Laptop Screen

Cracked laptop screen? Learn how to diagnose the issue and what steps to take — or bring it to Efix for fast professional replacement.

Step 1 — Diagnose

Connect your laptop to an external monitor. If the external screen works, the fault is likely the laptop display and not the GPU or motherboard.

Step 2 — Order the correct replacement

Match the exact model, size, connector, and resolution. A mismatch can result in no image or improper fit.

Step 3 — Replacement basics

Power off, remove power source and battery (if removable). Carefully detach bezel, unscrew the panel, disconnect the LVDS/eDP cable, replace, reconnect, and reassemble.

Step 4 — When to call Efix

If you’re unsure, stop — professional replacement avoids damage to cables, hinge assemblies and the motherboard. Efix uses OEM or high-grade compatible panels and tests before returning the device.
